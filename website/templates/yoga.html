Yoga


Bow Pose

Stand with your feet shoulder width apart. flex your knees and hips and sit back into the squat while lowering your body. Continue down to full depth. Return to starting position.

Child Pose

Begin in a tabletop position with your hands and knees on the ground. Sit back onto your heels and extend your arms out in front of you. Rest your forehead on the ground. Hold for a few breaths and then release.

Cobra Pose

Start prone, hands under shoulders. Inhale, lift chest, arch back. Elongate neck, gaze up. Breathe, hold. Release, exhale.

Dog Pose

Begin on your hands and knees with your hands shoulder-width apart and your knees hip-width apart.Spread your fingers wide and press down into your palms, making sure that your fingers are evenly spaced and facing forward.Tuck your toes under and lift your knees off the ground, straightening your legs as much as possible.

Vajrasana

Begin in a kneeling position with your knees together and your shins and feet on the floor. Sit back on your heels and place your palms face down on your thighs, with your fingers pointing towards your knees. Relax your shoulders and take deep breaths.
